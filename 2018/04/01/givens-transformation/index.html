Givens Transformation(rotation Transformation)

Apr 1, 2018

What is the Givens transformation? you might have encountered it dozen times without knowing its formal name. Very broadly speaking, Givens transformation , also known as Givens rotation, is a rotation transformation that performed in the plane spanned by two vectors. Let’s think about the rotation transformation in 2-D Euclidean space. As what you have learned in your linear algebra class , a linear transformation can be represent with a matrix.
