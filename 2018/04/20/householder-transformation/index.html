Householder Transformation(mirror/reflection)

Apr 20, 2018

Definition

Householder transformation ,also called mirror/reflection transformation, transforms vector \(x\) to vector \(y\), where \(x\) and \(y\) are symmetric with respect to axis \(e_i\), \(e_i\) commonly is an unit column vector. That’s reason why we call it mirror/reflection transformation. Noted that mirror and reflection transformation are actually equivalent from the mathematical view , the only difference is that how we look at them. Like before, let’s firstly consider Householder transformation in the 2-D Euclidean space, Given a vector \(x = (\xi_1, \xi_2)^T\) , how to get its mirror vector \(y\) with respect to \(e_1=(1,0)^T\)?
